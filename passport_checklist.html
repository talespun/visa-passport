Passport Services:
New Passport Checklist

VERY IMPORTANT: Once you complete the Passport Acceptance Agency (Courthouse or Post Office) visit, include this Sealed Package, along with a signed copy of this Checklist and the 2nd copy of the items identified with (Print 2 Copies). Place ALL items inside the Overnight FedEx package and put the Shipping Label on the outside of the envelope. Please call us at 877-503-9838 with questions.

Starting Instructions

* Please contact a Passport Acceptance Agent to schedule an in person visit to have your documents adjudicated. For the nearest location, please visit http://iafdb.travel.state.gov/

* NOTE: Do not sign the application or visit a Passport Acceptance Agent until instructed by a Passport Specialist.

* A signed Letter of Authorization must be included with the documents. This form is emailed once all documents have been reviewed with a Passport Specialist.

* The passport photo must meet strict government standards. Review 2. Passport Photo for photo specifics.

* Inspect the Check – Make sure that you have entered all information correctly including signing your check.

* If you order the Passport Card it will be delivered separately and can take an additional 2 to 4 weeks for delivery.

DS-11 Passport Application

* Complete Online through the government website link provided by RushMyPassport.

* The application is a “Smart Form.” Once you have entered all your personal information, it will apply all the data to the appropriate DS form to print.

* Print 2 copies (Single-Sided only).

* Hand-written applications are NOT accepted.

* Ensure bar codes are located at the bottom right hand corner of pages 1 & 2.

Passport Photo

* Color photo must be 2 x 2 inches and have a white background.

*Should be professionally taken within the past 6 months.

* The Passport Acceptance Agent will staple the photo onto the application.

* Have a full face front view (natural facial expression – please do not smile).

* No uniforms or headgear, as well as, no white shirt.

* “Selfie” style photos are not acceptable.

* Eyeglasses are no longer allowed to be worn in passport photos.

* If you wear religious headgear on a daily basis, you may wear this in your passport photo. You must submit a letter explaining that you wear this headgear daily for religious reasons. This headgear must not obscure your face.

Proof of U.S. Citizenship

* Must be an ORIGINAL or CERTIFIED copy of U.S. Birth Certificate (with both parents names on it).

* Must have a raised or embossed seal.

* Original Naturalization Certificate is acceptable.

* No Hospital Certificates/Abstracts or Photocopies.

* Original(s) will be returned by government.

* A Marriage Certificate or Divorce Decree is required if the last name has changed.

* Please note: An expired Passport can serve as Proof of US Citizenship.

VitalChek.com can help obtain an official certified Birth Certificate online quickly.

Proof of Identification

* A copy (front & back) of the valid ID must be included.

* Must be valid and issued GREATER than 6 months.

* ID can be a Drivers License, State ID, Military ID, or any Gov’t ID. Paper, Learner’s Permits and temporary licenses are not acceptable forms of ID.

* NOTE: If the name on the ID DOES NOT match the Proof of Citizenship – an original Name Change document (marriage certificate, divorce decree, or court ordered name change document) is required.

Government Fees

* Must be payable to the “US Department of State”.

* It must be included in the Sealed Envelope with your original documents.

* The amount of the check will only be either $170 or $200.

* A Starter or Temporary Check is NOT accepted.

* If using a personal check, a pre-printed Name and Address must be located in the upper left hand corner.

* Ensure that the applicant’s full name and date of birth is written in the memo section of the check or money order, if not pre-printed in the upper left hand corner

Travel Itinerary

* Most commonly used itineraries are airline, cruise & hotel reservations.

* A Business Letter on Original Company Letterhead can be used in lieu of travel itinerary.

* The Travel Itinerary and DS-11 application dates must match and include the applicant’s name.

* Print Two Copies for your order.

* PLEASE NOTE: A Business Letter cannot be used with a Same Day order request.

Call PassportVisaNow.com

* Before you have your documents sealed and shipped via FedEx, please contact our office for a final review.

* During this call, you will receive the Letter of Authorization and pre-paid shipping label.

* Our number is 877-503-9838 – Press 2

Letter of Authorization

* One copy must be included within your sealed documents. An additional copy must be inside your FedEx package.

* This Letter gives RushMyPassport permission to represent you at the U.S. Department of State.

* Hand print and sign in BLACK INK ONLY.

* Date requested on form is today’s date.

* Ensure that the Courier Company Name is printed as it appears at the top of that page. This will help avoid processing delays.

DO NOT VISIT THE PASSPORT ACCEPTANCE AGENT BEFORE YOU HAVE RECEIVED THE LETTER OF AUTHORIZATION.

Sealed Envelope – Passport Acceptance Agency Visit

* This will occur ONLY after you have spoken with a Passport Specialist.

* The documents will be witnessed, signed & sealed at a Courthouse or local Post Office and returned to you for submission to RushMyPassport.

* Please write the Applicant Name, Birth Date & Departure Date on outside of the Sealed Envelope.

* A $25.00 fee is separately charged for sealing your documents.

Pre-paid Overnight Shipping Label

* The Pre-paid Overnight Shipping Label is included in the Step 2 email

* It is in the same email with the Letter of Authorization.

* Print this label and place on the outside of an overnight shipping envelope.

* You will only receive this pre-paid shipping label once a Passport Specialist has ensured that you have collected all the necessary passport items for

submission to the U.S. Department of State.

Required Second Copies

VERY IMPORTANT:

* Do not forget to include the required second copies of DS-11 application, Letter of Authorization and Travel Itinerary. These items will be included

   in the FedEx package, along with the sealed envelope from the Passport Acceptance Agent. Failure will cause your order to be delayed.

* Your passport application may be denied if you are in arrears of child support, have been convicted of a felony, or have been notified by the IRS

   of owing back taxes.

Shipping The Passport Back To You

* If you request your passport to be delivered to a residential address, it DOES NOT require a signature for release at delivery.

* If you prefer a signature release of your passport, please provide RushMyPassport with a business address.
