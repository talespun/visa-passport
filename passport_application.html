Passport Services:
Passport Application Forms

If you discover that your passport is lost or if you need to renew an expired US passport, PassportVisaNow.com has the resources to renew passports fast. Use our chart below as a quick reference guide to the forms you will need for your passport application. Get instructions for filling out each form by clicking on the corresponding form number.

You must print these forms clearly on plain, 8.5″ x 11″ white paper using either a laser or inkjet printer in black ink in order to get a passport online.

New Passport

REQUIREMENTS:

ONE of the following must be true:

You are applying for your first U.S. passport.

You are 16 or older and your previous U.S. passport was issued when you were under age 16.

You are applying for your first U.S. passport.

Your previous U.S. passport was issued more than 15 years ago.

  • Form DS-1
  • Checklist

Child Passport

REQUIREMENTS:

The child is under 16 years of age.

BOTH parents must appear with child at a local acceptance agent.

If one of the parents or legal guardians cannot be present at the acceptance agent, then Form DS-3053 (Minor Consent Form) must be Notarized.

  • Form DS-11
  • Form DS-3053
  • Checklist

Lost/Stolen Passport

REQUIREMENTS:

Your previous U.S. passport was lost or stolen.

You must complete both the DS-11 and DS-64 (Statement Regarding Lost or Stolen Passport) forms.

  • Form DS-11
  • Form DS-3053
  • Checklist

Name Change

REQUIREMENTS:

If your passport is less than a year old, use the following form (DS-5504)

If your passport is older than a year, use the following form (DS-82)

  • Form DS-11
  • Form DS-3053
  • Checklist

Damaged Passport

REQUIREMENTS:

You must complete the DS-11 and the Statement Regarding Mutilated passport if your passport has been damaged but still in your possession.

  • Form DS-11
  • Mutilated forms
  • Checklist

Second Passport

REQUIREMENTS:

If you meet one of the following criteria you may request a second passport:

If you are a frequent traveler and need to obtain a visa stamp in your current valid 10 year passport while you are traveling abroad.

You are applying for your first U.S. passport.

If you are traveling between politically sensitive countries.

  • Form DS-82
  • Checklist

Second Passport

REQUIREMENTS: (Passport must be in your possession, not damaged, and issued when you were 16 or older). Your Most Recent U.S. Passport is one of the following:

Expiring or has expired in the last 5 yrs.

Low on blank passport pages.

Issued more than one year ago and legally changed your name.
