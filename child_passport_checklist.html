Lost Passport Checklist

VERY IMPORTANT: Once you complete the Passport Acceptance Agency (Courthouse or Post Office) visit, include this Sealed Package, along with a signed copy of this Checklist and the 2nd copy of the items identified with (Print 2 Copies). Place ALL items inside the Overnight FedEx package and put the Shipping Label on the outside of the envelope. Please call us at 877-503-9838 with questions.

Starting Instructions

* Please contact a Passport Acceptance Agent to schedule an in person visit to have your documents adjudicated. For the nearest location, please visit http://iafdb.travel.state.gov/

* NOTE: Do not sign the application or visit a Passport Acceptance Agent until instructed by a Passport Specialist.

* A signed Letter of Authorization must be included with the documents. This form is emailed once all documents have been reviewed with a Passport Specialist.

* The passport photo must meet strict government standards. Review 2. Passport Photo for photo specifics.

* Inspect the Check – Make sure that you have entered all information correctly including signing your check.

* If you order the Passport Card it will be delivered separately and can take an additional 2 to 4 weeks for delivery.

DS-11 Passport Application

* If your child has yet to receive a Social Security #, you must show documented paperwork that one has been requested.

* Complete Online only through the government website link provided in the Step 1 email.

* The application is a “Smart Form.” Once you have entered all your personal information, it will apply all the data to the appropriate DS form to print.

* Print out Single-Sided only.

* Hand-written applications are NOT accepted.

* Ensure bar codes are located at the bottom right hand corner of pages 1 & 2.

* On the upper left hand corner, ensure that there is a vertical scan code (below the American Eagle).

* Print 2 Copies for submission.

* NOTE: DO NOT SIGN OR VISIT A PASSPORT ACCEPTANCE AGENT UNTIL YOU CONTACT RushMyPassport FOR THE LETTER OF AUTHORIZATION.
