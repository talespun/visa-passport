About Us

About PassportVisaNow.com

PassportVisaNow.com is an industry leader in expediting U.S. passports. Our goal is to eliminate the time and hassle associated with getting a new passport or visa. From the moment we opened our doors, we have dedicated ourselves to providing U.S. citizens with their travel documents in a hurry. The process is easy and quick.


PassportVisaNow.com is owned by Expedited Travel, LLC, which is registered with U.S. Passport Agencies.


How is this possible?

Through our network of passport hand couriers registered with the U.S. Passport Agencies across the country, your U.S. passport is submitted and processed in as quickly as 24 hours.


The moment you place your order it will be routed to one of our highly trained U.S. passport and visa specialists who will email you detailed instructions, passport applications and documents. Our proprietary software will track your order through the entire process.


Why you should use us?

We utilize a national team of U.S. passport and visa specialists that undergo rigorous training to ensure a superior client experience. In order to give you peace of mind, we have developed proprietary software that will allow you to track your order through each step of the process from the moment it leaves your hands to the time it is delivered to your doorstep. It is our goal to provide each and every client with the secure and rapid delivery of his or her documents.
