Accepted forms of Primary and Secondary IDs are listed below. Make sure photocopies are very clear.

If Primary ID has been issued within the last 6 months, you must provide photocopies of 1 Primary ID and 5 Secondary IDs. Send additional ID with your application even if Acceptance Agent says they are not required.
