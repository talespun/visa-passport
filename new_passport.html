Guidelines for Expediting a First-Time or New Passport

  • You are a US citizen and:
  • You are a first time applicant age 18 or older
  • OR your most recent U.S. Passport was issued when you were under age 16
  • OR your previous U.S. passport was issued more than 15 years ago
